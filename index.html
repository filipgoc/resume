About me

I create modern learning experiences, be it online classes, in-person workshops or professional development courses. I designed an award-winning 3D Printer, taught in classrooms and ran hand-on workshops and MOOCs. I am a tinkerer and a DIY aficionado. I cherish the smell of freshly cut wood, but I am at home making things digital.

My approaches are steeped in cognitive sciences, design thinking, game-inspired learning, and both constructionist and constructivist approaches. I believe in curiosity, combining liberal arts with digital fabrication, and open-ended, learner-centered instruction.
